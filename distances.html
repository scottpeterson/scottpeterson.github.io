How are these distances calculated?

All distances shown are driving miles between school campuses. We've saved official NCAA distances over the years, but for pairs we don't have on file, we use OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine) — the same open-source routing engine behind many popular mapping apps — to estimate the drive.
