How We Measure Competitiveness

A% (AQ Bid %) represents a team's probability of earning their conference's automatic qualifier (AQ) to the NCAA Tournament based on 1,000+ season simulations. The most competitive conferences are those where multiple teams have meaningful A% values, indicating that the race for the automatic qualifier is still wide open. We rank conferences by considering the number of contenders (A% 20%), the spread between top teams, and the overall distribution of AQ probability across the conference.
