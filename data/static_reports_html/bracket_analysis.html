2026 D3 WBB NCAA Tournament Bracket Analysis

Sectional hosting, flight projections, win probabilities & path difficulty

The NCAA D3 tournament places 64 teams into 4 quadrants of 16. After two rounds, each quadrant produces 4 Sweet 16 teams that play at a single sectional host site. The NCAA prefers the highest seed to host, but the 500-mile rule means teams 500+ miles away require a flight (paid by NCAA). To minimize flights, a lower seed may host if it keeps more teams within driving distance. This report enumerates all possible 4-team sectionals per quadrant (accounting for tournament results), calculates their probability using efficiency-based win models, and determines the likely host and flight count for each scenario.

How tough is each team's bracket draw, independent of the team itself? The Total sums all remaining rounds — a team with a high total faces the hardest gauntlet regardless of its own strength. Unlike Win%, which rewards being dominant, this metric purely measures the opponents in your path.
