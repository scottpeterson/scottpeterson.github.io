Column Legend

Team Name of the college or university basketball team
Conf Conference affiliation of the team
Region NCAA tournament region the team belongs to
Massey Rank Team ranking according to Massey rating system
Eff Rank Team ranking based on efficiency metrics
NPI Rank Team ranking based on NCAA Power Index
Curr Season Results Rank Ranking based on current season performance
Strength of Record Measures how difficult a team's win-loss record is to achieve based on its schedule
Wins Above Bubble Measures how many more (or fewer) wins a team has compared to what an average bubble team would be expected to achieve against the same schedule
Preseason Rank Team's preseason ranking prediction
MEDIAN Median value across all ranking systems (not including preseason)
OVR_RANK Overall composite ranking position
Stdev Standard deviation across different ranking systems
