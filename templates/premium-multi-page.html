Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between the reports?

The Single Team Report is a weekly in-season report delivered every Monday from November through March. It covers your team's current stats, tournament odds, player impact, and upcoming game previews. The Offseason Report covers the offseason period with analysis and insights to keep you informed between seasons.
