16th Win Countdown

D3 Women's Basketball 2025-26 | Updated February 22, 2026

Why 16 Wins Matters: In NPI calculation, teams always count their top 15 wins. Once a team reaches 16 wins, they can potentially drop their lowest-value win if it would lower their NPI. The "Lowest Counting Win" shows the floor that could be raised with additional quality wins.

Projection Method: "Likely By" dates use cumulative probability modeling. We track the probability distribution of additional wins across all remaining games, and show the date when there's a 70% cumulative probability of reaching 16 (or 17) wins. This accounts for varying win probabilities across the schedule.
